iCoder started it's journey in 2016 with a simple vision: to create a space where anyone could learn coding in an accessible and engaging way. What began as a passion project has grown into a thriving community of learners and educators, united by a shared love for coding. Over the years, iCoder has evolved from a basic tutorial blog to a comprehensive learning platform, offering a wide range of resources, from beginner guides to advanced coding challenges. Through dedication and the support of our ever-growing community, iCoder has become a trusted source for coding education, and I’m excited to continue this journey, helping more people unlock the power of coding.
